Frequently Asked Questions

Is Bitcoin legal in the UK?

Yes! Bitcoin is completely legal in the UK. HMRC provides guidance on how to treat Bitcoin for tax purposes, and businesses are free to accept it as payment.

What if the price of Bitcoin goes up or down?

You can price your goods and services in pounds ‚Äî your Bitcoin wallet will automatically convert the payment to the correct amount in sats. Many businesses convert to fiat immediately, while others hold some Bitcoin as an investment.

What's the difference between Lightning and on-chain Bitcoin?

Lightning is perfect for everyday retail ‚Äî instant and cheap. On-chain is better for large amounts and long-term storage. We'll help you set up whichever suits your business.

How do I integrate with my point of sale?

There are various options depending on your setup ‚Äî from simple QR codes to dedicated POS apps like Breez. We'll recommend the best solution for your specific setup.

Do I need to worry about tax?

HMRC treats Bitcoin like other assets or currencies. You'll pay income tax and VAT just like with any other payment method. We can point you to official guidance.

How do I let customers know I accept Bitcoin?

We'll list your business on btcmap.org ‚Äî the go-to directory for Bitcoin-accepting businesses. We'll also share your business on our social media channels to the UK Bitcoin community.
